Future Plans

Through university and work experiences, I have found that I really enjoy programming and being creative. I was originally open to a future in research, but I have since realised that writing formal papers is nowhere near as exciting as building something new.

I am interested in software engineering and data science. My major is artificial intelligence and data analytics, so my long-term career path was to become a machine learning engineer. I find backend development fun, except constructing SQL queries is boring. Frontend is very creaive, but managing the little intricacies of HTML and CSS is incredibly tedious.

My past experience has given me a solid understanding of what I would like to do in the future, however I am looking for a software engineering internship at a big company for summer 2020/21. I have yet to experience collaborative development at a company, because my current role is smilar to a freelancer and my Data61 internship was an individual project under one supervisor.
